Hello again!

Great to have you here, my name is Peter Akajiobi and I am a Nigerian-American UX  Designer in Los Angeles CA.  

I happen to be one of those people that loves to express both their creative and analytical sides . I am fortunate enough to have discovered the wonderful world of UX design.

Growing up with an insatiable curiosity, I was fascinated by cars, I created(at least i thought i did) this game where I would say every nice car that drove by was my car, I played this game with my brother and friends, we sat outside for hours watching cars drive by, the more I played the game I wondered why there so many different brands of cars, they all do the same thing right? My desire to understand the needs/motivation of users was cultivated at an early age and that serves me now in my career in UX design.

With a background in sales, I have extensive experience in understanding the needs of the consumer  and business. I believe a great UX design can help a product stand out in a crowded marketplace, maintain/attract customers and drive superior business results.

Technology has revolutionized how people learn, interact and create. I am passionate about my part in shaping digital products and services that improve the HUMAN experience.

Thank you for stopping by my portfolio, I would love to connect with you and hear what you think or we could talk about our love for cars, new trends in design, avocado toast, the US Space Force, new adventures...Cheers!
